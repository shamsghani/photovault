# PhotoVault

A lightweight, privacy-focused photo archive and browsing application designed for organizing, exploring, and preserving large collections of scanned photographs.

## Features

* 🖼️ High-resolution photo viewing
* 🏷️ Custom tagging system for photo organization
* 🔍 Fast image browsing and filtering
* 📁 Local-first design
* 📸 Support for scanned family photographs and historical archives
* 💾 Metadata stored in simple JSON format
* 🌐 Browser-based interface with no installation required
* ⚡ Lightweight and fast
* 🔒 Privacy-friendly — your photos stay under your control

## Why PhotoVault?

Many modern photo management platforms are designed around cloud storage, subscriptions, and social sharing. PhotoVault focuses on a different goal: helping individuals and families preserve, organize, and explore personal photo collections, especially large archives of scanned prints and historical photographs.

Whether you're digitizing family albums, preserving genealogy records, archiving historical imagery, or building a personal digital museum, PhotoVault provides a simple and efficient way to manage your collection.

## Use Cases

* Family photo archives
* Genealogy and ancestry projects
* Historical photograph collections
* Museum and community archives
* Personal image libraries
* Photo digitization projects
* Long-term digital preservation

## Technology

Built with:

* HTML5
* CSS3
* Vanilla JavaScript
* JSON-based metadata storage
* GitHub Pages deployment

No backend. No database. No tracking.
